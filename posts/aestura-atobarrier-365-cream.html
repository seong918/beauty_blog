How to use it (without wasting it)

Routine order trips people up, so here's the no-fuss version for a cream like this:

  1. Use it as your last leave-on step at night (before SPF in the morning).
  2. Warm a pea-sized amount between your fingers and press it over the face.
  3. Add a little extra on dry patches, and don't drag it.
  4. Layer it over a hydrating toner or serum underneath for the most out of it.
