How to use it (without wasting it)

Routine order trips people up, so here's the no-nonsense version for this format:

  1. Cleanse first, then work thinnest to thickest texture.
  2. Spritz onto clean skin (or layer it under your serum) and press in. Don't rub.
  3. Re-mist through the day whenever skin feels tight. That's the entire point of a mist.
  4. Seal it with moisturizer so the water doesn't just evaporate off your face.

One safety note: it's a fine spray you're aiming near your face, so if you have asthma or reactive airways, spritz onto clean hands and press in, or mist from a good arm's length.
