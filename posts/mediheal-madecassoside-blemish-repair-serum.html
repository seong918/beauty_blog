How to use it without wasting it

Layering order trips people up, so here's the no-nonsense version for a serum like this:

  1. Cleanse first, then move thinnest to thickest texture.
  2. Apply 2–3 drops to clean, slightly damp skin before moisturizer.
  3. Wait a minute for it to sink in before the next layer.
  4. Use AM and/or PM, and seal with moisturizer (plus SPF in the morning).
