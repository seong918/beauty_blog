How to use it (without wasting it)

Layering order is where people go wrong, so, for this format:

  1. Use as your last leave-on step at night (before SPF in the morning).
  2. Warm a pea-sized amount between fingers and press over the face.
  3. Layer a little more on dry patches; don't drag.
  4. Pair with a hydrating toner/serum underneath for best results.
